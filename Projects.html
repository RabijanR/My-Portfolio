My Projects

Here are some of the projects I have worked on. Some were completed during my first year in engineering, while others reflect my current Networking and IT focus.

Design Project — Scissor Lift (Engineering Year 1)

Designed and 3D modeled a functioning scissor lift. The lift had to be stable and meet specifications. My group went further by adding color, reflection, and texture.

Fixing a Non-Functioning Drill (Engineering Year 1)

Disassembled and repaired a broken drill. Since we were successful, we did not need to prepare the presentation required for groups that failed.

Fixing a Broken Network (Networking)

Troubleshot a Packet Tracer topology in Intro to Networking 2. I identified and fixed issues in the network devices so they could communicate again.

Creating a School Management Program (Networking/IT)

Built a system to keep track of students and their grades. It included a GUI made with Python’s Tkinter library.
